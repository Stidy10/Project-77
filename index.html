Console Screen Project on console Visual Studio is an application you can use to code and creat projects. To instal Visual Studio, you just have tolook it up on any search engine and select the correct link. Then choose the downloading type(mac,android...). Visual Studio is used because it supports losts of libraries and languages. To open console screen, once you have finished your project, you press 'go live' at the bottom of the coding platform. Afterwards, right click and press 'inspect' at the top of the inspection page, you will see a 'console' button, click on it. You are now in the console screen. To print things on the console screen, you use 'console.log("what ever you want to say");'.
